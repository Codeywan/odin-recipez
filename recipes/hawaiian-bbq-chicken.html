Hawaiian BBQ Chicken

Description

Hawaiian BBQ Chicken like they sell at L&L Hawaiian BBQ

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs chicken thighs

    • Marinade:
  • 3 cups soy sauce
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp minced ginger
  • 1/4 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix everything together and pour over chicken in bowl or bag to marinate.
  2. Marinate minimum 4 hours.
  3. Cook on stove over medium heat until fully cooked.
