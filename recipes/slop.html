Susan's Slop

Description

Susan's slop recipe card found on reddit or something

Ingredients

  • Elbow or Shell Macaroni (1lb?)
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 onion chopped
  • garlic salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • cayenne pepper
  • 1 14.5 oz can tomatoes
  • 1 14.5 oz can tomato sauce

Steps

  1. boil macaroni until done by box directions
  2. brown the ground beef and onion
  3. drain the fat. add seasonings and tomato stuff.
  4. simmer for a while
  5. add macaroni.
    6. "Great Shit" - Susan
